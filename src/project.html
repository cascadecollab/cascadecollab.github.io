Project Details

Each artist will draw a segment of the artwork in their own style, one character per artist, with the only exception being the two characters hugging in segment 6.
Therefore, there are a total of 14 slots, rather than 15.

The artists will be compensated for their work using their standard rate for commissions, unless a different price is negotiated individually.
