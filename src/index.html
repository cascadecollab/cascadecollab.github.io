What is Cascade Collab?

Cascade Collab is a collaborative art project where multiple artists contribute to a vertically stacked artwork, each drawing a segment in their own unique style. Inspired by the iconic Durarara! ending meme, the goal is to showcase artistic diversity in a seamless flow.

Want to Participate?

Whether you're a well-known artist with a big following or a niche artist with just a few happy followers, this is a space for all artists to shine. By bringing together diverse styles, we hope to not only create something amazing but also help smaller artists gain visibility.
